Frequently Asked Questions
View All
How do I get started?
Begin with a short online evaluation — it takes about ten minutes. A licensed physician reviews your answers, and if treatment is right for you, your protocol ships directly to your door.
Who qualifies for treatment?
Adults 18 and over who complete our medical intake. Your physician reviews your health history, goals, and any current medications to confirm treatment is safe and appropriate for you.
Is my information private?
Always. Your data is encrypted, never sold, and visible only to your care team. Deliveries arrive in plain packaging with a discreet billing descriptor.
What does treatment cost?
Plans start with a free physician consultation. Pricing depends on your protocol and medication, and is always shown upfront — no hidden fees, cancel anytime.
What happens after I begin?
Your care team checks in monthly, reviews your progress, and adjusts your protocol as your body responds. You can message your physician at any time.
